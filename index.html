About Us

Who We Are

Schools for Africa is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that strives to bring sustainable eduction to children and communities in impoverished regions of Africa.

Our Mission:

Our goal is to aid these children in escaping the shackles of poverty by providing them with a brighter future through education.

Meet the Founder

Eleanor Su

The concept of Schools for Africa Fund was ideated by Eleanor Su when she was ten years old. In 2009, Eleanor first learned about the Sierra Leone civil war through Free the Children’s California Take Action Academy. She was deeply touched by a particular image of a young Sierra Leonean boy who had been a victim of the conflict--one of his arms had been severed by a machete. She was shocked to discover that due to this conflict, communities and schools were destroyed and thousands of children were forced to become children soldiers. This moment inspired Eleanor to take action and work towards making a difference in the lives of these children. Since 2009, Schools for Africa Fund has raised more than $42,000 to build four schools in Makeni, Sierra Leone through its international partners. The organization fundraises primarily through local fundraisers such as annual benefit concerts, bake sales, concessions stands, and run-a-thons, as well as through grants and corporate sponsors. She has established branches of the organization in both her local middle and high schools. Additionally, in 2012, Eleanor traveled to Kenya and worked on the foundation of Oleleshwa Secondary School through the organization Free the Children.

Impact


Schools for Africa Fund strives to empower North American youth to make a difference in the lives of others in developing countries. Through our various fundraisers and awareness-raising events, we work towards with our international partners on our projects to impact communities at large in impoverished regions of Africa, particularly the Makeni community of Sierra Leone and the Darfur region in Sudan. To ensure transparency, we frequently update our audiences with detailed reports and pictures of our ongoing and past projects.
