BMo finishes T5 at Nationals 2018

After losing the pool by one point in a three way tie to Pittsburg and Washington, BMo (4) lost to Oregon (3) in a heated quarterfinal showdown, 15-13.

BMo takes back the Region

BMo goes undefeated on the weekend to secure the title of New England Regional Champions. We defeat UMass 15-10 in the finals, sending us to Nationals for the first time in 11 years.

BMo defeats Defending Champions, Carleton
