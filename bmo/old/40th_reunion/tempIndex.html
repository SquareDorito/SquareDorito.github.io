40th Reunion Events


The 2015-2016 academic year represents the 40th season of Brown Ultimate! To celebrate this exciting milestone, the program will host two reunion events over the course of the year, and would like to invite all interested alumni to participate in the festivities. These events -- Huck a Hunk of Burning Pumpkin and Commencement Weekend -- are described in detail below. If you are interested in attending one or both of these events, please fill out the form at the bottom of this page.


Huck a Hunk of Burning Pumpkin


Date: October 23-25, 2015
Location: Tournament Location TBD, and Brown University

Huck a Hunk is the annual Brown Ultimate fall home tournament. In past years, HHBP has served as a reunion weekend for alumni who have graduated recently enough to be aware of the tournament. This year, we would like to invite everyone who has played a role in shaping our amazing program to join us in the fall to kick off celebration of the team's 40th season. This weekend will include the traditional BMo vs. alumni game at the tournament, at least one alumni dinner, and plenty of opportunities to meet fellow alumni and cheer for the current BMo team as we take on regional competitors on our home turf.

Rough Schedule:
  • Friday 10/23
    • 5pm: BMo showcase game against a regional competitor, to take place on campus
    • 7pm: Alumni dinner with introductions & gifts
  • Saturday 10/24
    • 9am: Tournament begins at location TBD
    • 7pm: Potential second alumni dinner
    • 10pm: Tournament party
  • Sunday 10/25
    • 9am: Tournament games resume
    • 3pm: Tournament finals


Commencement Weekend


Date: May 27-29, 2016
Location: Brown University

Brown has agreed to recognize and honor the ultimate program and its history during the University's official 2016 commencement and reunion weekend events. Exact details are still being hashed out; however, the weekend is likely to include a University hosted lunch, an alumni frisbee game, introductions and awards from the current team, a presentation of the long and celebrated history of Ultimate Frisbee at Brown, and more. Further information will be sent out to those who are interested as it becomes available.

Costs and Donations


We are asking for donations of $100 per attendee per weekend. This money will go towards meals and activities at the events, and will also cover commemorative shirts and frisbees. The team will gratefully accept donations in excess of this amount; any additional funds will help lower the costs of playing ultimate at Brown for interested students.
