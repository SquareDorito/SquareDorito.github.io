About Us

For over 40 years, the Brown Ultimate program has churned out elite players and coaches all across the country. Brown Ultimate, commonly referred to as “The Program,” claims two men’s national titles (1999 and 2005) and four* Callahan winners. This year’s BMo has relied on contributions from a strong Junior class, which makes up most of its offensive line, and a standout group of sophomores headed by 2018 Rookie of the Year John Randolph. Last year, BMo returned to the College Championships after a 10-year hiatus, thanks to its youth and the superior coaching of Sam Lehman and Jake Smart, whose commitment and acuity have taken BMo from kangaroo court to nationals contender.


*not including Mac Hecht (2019 projected)
