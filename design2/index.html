Are you having emotional suffering? Are you having trouble functioning in your work or in your relationships? I will try to understand what you are struggling with and together, over time, we will figure out what you need.

It can be hard to look at oneself. I will try to help you with that. Often feelings of fear, shame, and guilt make it difficult. I will be very interested in understanding what you are contending with. I have many tools that could be helpful to you in understanding you and conducting your therapy.

I invite you to look through my website and contact me with any questions you have.
